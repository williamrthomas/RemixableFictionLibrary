This is an administrative tool for directly importing books from Standard Ebooks. All Standard Ebooks content is in the public domain with CC0 licensing for editorial enhancements.

Standard Ebooks Import Form

Enter the URL path identifier from Standard Ebooks, e.g., "jane-austen/pride-and-prejudice"

How to Find the URL Identifier

  1. Visit Standard Ebooks
  2. Find the book you want to import
  3. Click on the book to go to its detail page
  4. Look at the URL in your browser's address bar
  5. The URL will look like: https://standardebooks.org/ebooks/jane-austen/pride-and-prejudice
  6. The part after "/ebooks/" is the URL identifier: jane-austen/pride-and-prejudice
